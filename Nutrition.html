Nutrition Counselling

1. What is Nutrition & Why is it Important?

Nutrition is about eating a balanced diet to fuel your body. Good nutrition helps:

  • Maintain brain, muscle, bone, nerves, skin, blood circulation, and immune system.
  • Protect against diseases like heart disease, diabetes, cancer, and osteoporosis.

2. What Are Healthy Foods?

For optimal health, science supports a plant-based diet like:

  • Mediterranean-DASH Diet (Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension)
  • MIND Diet (Mediterranean-DASH Diet for Neurodegenerative Delay)

Plant-based diets reduce the risk of heart disease, cancer, and chronic illnesses.

3. Should You Take Daily Vitamins?

Vitamins and minerals are essential. Some common supplements include:

  • Vitamins A, B, C, D, E, K
  • Minerals like Calcium and Iron
  • Multivitamins for overall health

4. Do You Need a Daily Supplement?

For most healthy adults, a balanced diet provides all essential nutrients. However, supplements may be necessary for:

  • Vitamin D deficiency
  • Iron deficiency
  • Pregnant women needing folic acid

5. Nutrition for Older Adults

As we age, proper nutrition becomes even more important:

  • Boosts energy levels
  • Prevents age-related diseases like heart disease, cancer, and diabetes
  • Supports bone and muscle health
